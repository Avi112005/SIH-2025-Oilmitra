NMEO-OS (National Mission on Edible Oils)

Subsidy on seeds and fertilizers for oilseed farmers. Covers up to 50% of input costs. Applicable for Groundnut, Mustard, and Soybean cultivation.

💰 Potential Benefit: ₹9,250/hectare

PMFBY (Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana)

Comprehensive crop insurance scheme protecting farmers against crop losses due to natural calamities, pests, and diseases. Premium: 2% for farmers.

🛡️ Coverage: Up to ₹2,50,000 per hectare

State Seed Subsidy Program

Direct subsidy on certified seeds for improved crop varieties. Up to 40% discount on quality seeds. Ensures better germination and yield.

🌾 Savings: ₹2,000-3,000/hectare
