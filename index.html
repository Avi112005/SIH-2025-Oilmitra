Government Schemes

NMEO-OS (National Mission on Edible Oils)

Subsidy on seeds and fertilizers for oilseed farmers. Covers up to 50% of input costs.

PMFBY (Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana)

Comprehensive crop insurance scheme protecting farmers against crop losses due to natural calamities.

State Seed Subsidy Program

Direct subsidy on certified seeds for improved crop varieties. Up to 40% discount on quality seeds.
